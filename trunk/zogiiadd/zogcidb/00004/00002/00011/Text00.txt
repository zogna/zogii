    Family: Coccinellidae

    Subfamily: Chilocorinae

    Tribe: Chilocorini

    Length: 4-5 mm

     

    The genus Chilocorus is primarily Neotropical, but seven species are found in the Nearctic.

Chilocorus stigma is widespread over much of North America, though it is not found in the far West. The species is found as far north as Saskatchewan and Nova Scotia.

Adults are active early in the year, and both adults and larvae are predacious on scale insects and mealybugs.
