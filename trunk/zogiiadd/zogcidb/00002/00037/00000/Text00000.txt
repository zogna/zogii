

Description
Length 5.1-5.6 mm. Head, central part of pronotum and elytra black with slight bluish tint, shiny; margins of pronotum, venter of prothorax and legs reddish orange; pterothorax and abdomen almost entirely black; antenna with scape reddish and remaining segments infuscate. Dorsum covered by a short and recumbent golden pubescence. Pronotal disc very uneven with discrimen complete and smaller admedian and extensive lateral concave areas. Elytra densely, confusedly punctate, margins hardly visible from above; epipleuron narrow, incomplete at apical one third. Male: 5th and 6th abdominal ventrite emarginate medially; 5th ventrite with lateral setose foveae.
