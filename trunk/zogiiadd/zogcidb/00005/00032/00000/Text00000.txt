

Description
Length 1.2-1.3 mm. Head dark brown, pronotum and elytra brown usually with darker elongate spot on central part of each elytron, appendages yellowish brown. Dorsum covered by a pile of yellowish, moderately long pubescence that is erect or slightly inclined and intermixed on elytra. Pronotal disc moderately convex medially with dorsal punctures about as large as eye facets and 1-3 diameters apart, interspaces shiny; lateral borders not thickened, with fine bead. Elytra 2.5 times as long as pronotum, oval, narrowing apically; disc with setigerous punctures slightly larger than those on pronotum 1-2 diameters apart; lateral margins narrow but entirely visible; epipleuron narrow, incomplete at very apex. Male: 5th abdominal ventrite arcuate without setose areas; hind margin bordered by a groove.
