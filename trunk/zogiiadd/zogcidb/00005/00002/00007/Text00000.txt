

Description
Length 1.3-1.5 mm. Pitch black dorsally and ventrally; elytra with a yellowish or ochreous dot in the middle; trochanters and femora brown, tibiae and tarsi dirty yellowish. Winged; body shape hemispherical; pronotal margins visible from above, pronotum entirely finely rimmed along margins; elytra with conspicuous swelling near humeral edge; elytral margins with narrow rim, visible from above in anterior half. Head, pronotal and elytral surface shiny; head virtually impunctate, covered in long sparse setae. Pronotum covered in sparse fine punctures and long sparse setae. Scutellum elongate, triangular, flat, shiny, glabrous. Elytra impunctate with a few long setae in basal third and a row of evenly spaced setae along margin. Head with moderately large eyes, separated by 2.1 times eye width; flattened medially. Clypeus flat, long, straight anteriorly. Terminal maxillary palpomere and labial palpomere not observed. Antenna 9-segmented, scape expanded apically, club elongate, inner margin concave, apex angular. Prosternum mat, surface weakly furrowed, sparsely setose; mesosternum mat, transversely furrowed; metasternum shiny, impunctate, sparsely setose. Elytral epipleuron brown, broad, flat and perpendicular to elytron in anterior half, with some long setae, narrowing to a mere rim in apical half. Anterior margin of profemur strongly angled in the middle. Tarsus 4-segmented. Abdomen: ventrite 1 large, shiny and glabrous, ventrites 2-5 small, mat, transversely furrowed and bearing a few long setae. Last ventrite not grooved laterally.
