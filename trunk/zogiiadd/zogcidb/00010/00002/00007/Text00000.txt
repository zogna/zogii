 
Size: 5 - 7mm 
Basic colour: orange 
Pattern colour: black spots 
Number of spots: 11 
Spot fusions: rare 
Melanic (black) form: no 

Pronotum: orange 
Leg colour: brown 
Habitat: low herbage 
Host plant: white bryony 
Overwintering: low herbage 
Food: leaves of white bryony and other curcurbits 

Other notes: Recent immigrant, first recorded in May 1997. Wing cases covered in short downy hairs. The spread of this ladybird northwards in Europe may be due to global warming.
