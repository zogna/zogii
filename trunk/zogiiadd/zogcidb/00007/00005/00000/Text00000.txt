

Description
Length 1.2 mm. Dorsum and venter brown; appendages yellowish-brown; surfaces covered with short and slightly decumbent yellowish pubescence. Pronotum and elytral edges weakly crenulate, coarsely punctate, punctures irregular and variable in size, the elytral punctures distinctly larger than the pronotal ones. Elytral epipleuron very narrow and incomplete at apical one fourth. Ovipositor and spermatheca as figured.
