Size: 5 - 7mm 
Basic colour: orange-red 
Pattern colour: black spots 
Number of spots: 7-15 
Spot fusions: rare 
Melanic (black) form: rare 

Pronotum: white with black pattern 
Leg colour: black 
Habitat: wetlands 
Host plant: reeds 
Overwintering: unknown 
Food: aphids 

Other notes: A rare and noteworthy ladybird that dies out in Britain and then recolonises from Europe. Distinctly elongate in shape and somewhat flattened compared to the 7-spot ladybird.
