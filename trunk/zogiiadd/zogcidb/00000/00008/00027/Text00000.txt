

Size: 6 - 8mm 
Basic colour: red 
Pattern colour: black spots 
Number of spots: 5-11 
Spot fusions: rare 
Melanic (black) form: no 

Pronotum: black with anterior-lateral white marks 
Leg colour: black 
Habitat: varied but close to ant nests 
Host plant: various 
Overwintering: various, but close to ant nests 
Food: aphids 

Other notes: Rarely recorded far from nests of Formica ants. Can be distinguished from the 7-spot by more domed shape and presence of small white triangular marks on the underside below both the middle and hind legs (the 7-spot has such marks only below the middle legs).
